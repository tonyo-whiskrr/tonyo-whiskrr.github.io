How it works

From idea to working AI app in minutes

No configuration files. No API keys to wrangle. No model selection menus. Just describe what you need and Whiskrr handles the rest.

Step 01

Describe your app

Tell Whiskrr what your assistant should do — in plain language. Choose from ready-made templates for your industry, or start from scratch. No technical knowledge needed.

Step 02

Upload your knowledge

Drop in your documents, PDFs, spreadsheets, or paste website URLs. Whiskrr ingests and indexes everything so your AI assistant knows your business — not just the internet.

Step 03

Go live instantly

Test it, preview it, then flip it live with one click. Your AI is ready to answer questions, run scheduled automations, send notifications — reliably, every time.
